## Find the best vehicle for your  trip

This application can be used to help you find the car that fits your needs to the trip that you are going on.

* Distance
* Gas Price
* Total funds for gasoline
* Number of Cylinders 
  * 4, 6 or 8
* Displacement
* Gross Horsepower
* Transmission
  * Automatic or Manual
